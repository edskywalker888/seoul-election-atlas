# Seoul Halloween crowd crush — English Wikipedia (captured)

## Basics
- Date: 2022-10-29 ~22:15 KST
- Location: Itaewon alley adjacent to Hamilton Hotel, Yongsan-gu (45m long, 3.2m at narrowest)
- Casualties: **159 deaths** (133 Korean, 26 foreign), 196–197 injured

## Government response
- Yoon: emergency briefing, addressed nation next morning
- Interior Minister Lee Sang-min apologized; Police Chief Yoon Hee-keun: "information management was insufficient"
- Candlelight vigils; signs reading "Step down, Yoon Suk-yeol"
- **Nov 2022 poll: 7 of 10 South Koreans believed government was responsible**
- 57% found investigation insufficient; majority demanded Lee Sang-min resignation

## Investigation outcomes
- 137 police officers deployed (vs 6,500 for nearby protest)
- 11+ emergency calls about crowd danger 4+ hours before; 79+ calls between 18:34 and incident
- 23 officers charged with manslaughter / negligence
- Feb 2023: Interior Minister Lee Sang-min suspended by National Assembly
- Sept 2024: 3 officers convicted of negligence (up to 3 years prison)
