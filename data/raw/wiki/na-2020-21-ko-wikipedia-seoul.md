# 제21대 국회의원 선거 서울특별시 — Korean Wikipedia (Seoul subpage, captured)

## Basics
- Date: 2020-04-15
- Seoul constituencies: 49 (one more than 2024; 노원구 was split into 갑/을/병)

## Seoul party-level totals
- 더불어민주당 (DPK): 41 seats
- 미래통합당 (UFP): 8 seats

## UFP-won districts (8)
- 용산구 (권영세 47.80%)
- 서초구 갑 (윤희숙 62.60%)
- 서초구 을 (박성중 53.66%)
- 강남구 갑 (태구민 58.40%)
- 강남구 을 (박진 50.94%)
- 강남구 병 (유경준 65.38%)
- 송파구 갑 (김웅 51.20%)
- 송파구 을 (배현진 50.46%)

## Notable DPK holds in close districts
- 용산구 came close: Gang Tae-ung (DPK) 47.14% vs 권영세 47.80% — 0.66pp margin
- 송파구 병 went DPK (남인순 52.48%)

## Notes for researchers
- Full per-district table captured; turnout per district not included in source
- Party-of-note: the runner-up in 관악구 갑 was 김성식 (independent, 33.55%), not a UFP candidate
- UFP (미래통합당) was formed Feb 2020 by merging 자유한국당 and related conservative forces; renamed to 국민의힘 (PPP) in Sept 2020
