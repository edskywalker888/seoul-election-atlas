> "윤석열 정부의 국정 실책과 여러 논란으로, 국정지지도에 대한 긍정 평가가 20%대를 기록한 뒤, 계속해서 낮은 수준을 유지"

### 2023 Gangseo-gu ward by-election as harbinger
Korean text treats the October 2023 Gangseo-gu defeat as early proof that "정권 심판 여론이 현실화" (government-punishment sentiment crystallized).

### First-ever opposition solo majority
> "여당이 아닌 야당에서 단독 과반이 나온 최초의 선거" — first time in Korean history that a non-ruling party holds a solo majority.

## Democratic Party / Lee Jae-myung context
- Third straight landslide: 2022 presidential → 21st assembly (180) → 22nd (175)
- Factional tension: pro-Lee vs anti-Lee; arrest-warrant vote passed Sept 2023
- Defection: 이낙연 (Lee Nak-yeon) left to form 새로운미래
- Geographic pattern: wins in Seoul 한강 belt, Gyeonggi semiconductor belt, Honam 28/28, Jeju 3/3; weak in 낙동강 belt (Busan/Ulsan/South Gyeongsang 5)

### Official framing
- 이해찬 (election committee chair): Democratic win reflected "윤석열 정부를 심판해야 한다는 뜻" (voters' will to hold Yoon accountable)
- 김부겸: "무능력, 무책임을 심판하자는 국민적 열망" (popular will to punish incompetence and irresponsibility)

## People Power Party collapse
- 108 seats — kept the 100-seat amendment veto floor
- 한동훈 (Han Dong-hoon, emergency committee chair) resigned: "민심은 언제나 옳다"
- 홍준표 (Daegu mayor): "깜도 안 되는 것을 데리고 와서 총선을 지휘" (blistering critique of campaign leadership)
- 안철수 (Bundang 갑 winner): "윤석열 대통령의 국정 운영이 국민들의 눈높이에 맞지 않았"

## Third parties
- **조국혁신당 (Cho Kuk / Rebuilding Korea):** 12 PR seats; center of 2019 prosecutor conflict; became second opposition party without any constituency candidates; demanded investigation of Kim Keon-hee
- **개혁신당 (Reform Party):** Led by 이준석 (former PPP chairman); critiqued Yoon directly
- **진보당 (Progressive Party):** 1 seat (Ulsan North)
- **새로운미래 (New Future):** 1 seat (Kim Jong-min, 세종 갑); Lee Nak-yeon himself lost (Gwangju Gwangsan)
- **녹색정의당 (Green-Justice):** 0 seats; Shim Sang-jeong retired after 25 years
